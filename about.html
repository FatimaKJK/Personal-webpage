Who is she?

Here's a short timeline of Fatima's life:

  • Fatima K.J. Kamara was born on October 8th, 1998 in Riverdale, Georgia.
  • Her family migrated from Sierra Leone, West Africa, giving her the experience of being first-generation African-American.
  • In elementary school, she was recognized as a "gifted" student and skipped 4th grade.
  • Fatima graduated high school with a 4.4 weighted GPA on a 4.0 scale as a result of taking IB and AP classes. She gave a speech at her graduation.
  • Fatima graduated from Johns Hopkins University in May of 2020 with a degree in Public Health.
  • She is currently studying to become a UX Designer, learning web development skills, and working on a plant-based product line.
  • In her spare time, she likes to travel, paint, garden, read, skate, and practice yoga.
  • Fatima has been a plant-based vegan since October of 2018.
  • Fun Fact: She is very enthusiastic about holistic healing and is building an at-home apothecary full of diverse botanicals.
